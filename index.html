About me

Design that looks right and works right.

I'm Elijah Olaniyi — a graphic designer, brand strategist, and web developer who helps growing businesses turn a good idea into a brand people remember. My work sits at the intersection of visual design and strategy: I don't just make things look good, I make sure they say the right thing to the right people.

I've partnered with startups building their first identity and established teams ready for a refresh, delivering everything from logo systems to fully coded websites. What sets me apart is range — most designers hand off a file and hope it translates online; I design it and build it myself, so nothing gets lost between the mockup and the browser.

If you're looking for a partner who can think in brand strategy and ship in working code, let's talk.
